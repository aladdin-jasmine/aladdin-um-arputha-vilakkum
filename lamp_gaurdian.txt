In the palace archives of Agrabah, a scroll once described the true power of the magic lamp.

The Genie, fearing the knowledge could be abused, removed the scroll from the modern records.

But ancient magic cannot be erased so easily.

Somewhere in the old revisions of this repository lies the secret phrase used to awaken the lamp.

Only those brave enough to explore the past will discover it.
