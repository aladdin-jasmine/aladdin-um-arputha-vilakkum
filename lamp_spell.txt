In the ancient city of Agrabah, Aladdin once discovered a mysterious lamp hidden among forgotten treasures.
When he rubbed the lamp, the mighty Genie appeared and granted wishes beyond imagination.

But the Genie warned Aladdin of a powerful secret spell that must never fall into the wrong hands.
To protect it, the spell was written in a developer’s scroll and later erased from the latest version.

Yet magic never truly disappears… it only hides in the history of time.

Those who are wise enough to explore the past will uncover the forgotten spell of the lamp.
